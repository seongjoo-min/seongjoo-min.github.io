I am a Ph.D. candidate in economics at the University of California, Berkeley. My research interests are econometrics of network models and empirical industrial organization. Before joining the Ph.D. program, I studied economics and applied mathematics at the University of California, Berkeley.

In August 2020, I will join the Tippie College of Business in the University of Iowa as an assistant professor.
