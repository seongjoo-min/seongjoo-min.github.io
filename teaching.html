Teaching

I have served as a graduate student instructor (teaching assistant) for undergraduate and graduate courses in econometrics, applied econometrics, and industrial organization.

Econometrics and Applied Econometrics (with Professor Bryan S. Graham):
  • Econometric Analysis (Econ 141)
  • Applied Econometrics and Public Policy (Econ 142)
  • Econometrics (Econ 240A)
 Covered Topics: Average Treatment Effect, Instrumental Variable Regression, Quantile Regression, Survival Analysis, Bayesian Bootstrapping, Basic Asymptotic Theory.


Industrial Organization (with Professor Kei Kawai):
  • Industrial Organization and Public Policy (Econ 121)
 Covered Topics: Pricing Strategies, Bundling, Basic Game Theory, Basic Auction Theory.
